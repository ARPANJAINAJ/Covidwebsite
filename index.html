things you should DO

wash your hand

Frequently wash your hands properly with sanitiser or hand-wash and keep sanitiser in pocket so that you can wash anytime anywhere

always wear a mask

the major safety precaution is wearing mask everytime,everywhere until you find a place where you are completely isolated

stay home during fever

If u catch fever,avoid going out and meeting with people and take medicine at home only and if condition get a little worse,then only move to hospital

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Whether it's your own pet or any street dog,please stay away from them as they are immune to this virus but they may act as carrier of this virus

avoid close contact with peoples

This is the key to win over this virus

avoid crowded area

Don't organise or attend any public gatherings until this virus is here.
